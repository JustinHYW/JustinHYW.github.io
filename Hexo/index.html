Problems that I have encountered

  • Port 4000 is already in use. the best solution is to restart your computer. Port 4000 is a localhost. if it is occupied it means something else is in use, you can either kill it or just to restart.
  • image won’t work. when i was adding my image into the blog, I alwasy got this message “the image cannot loaded”. So after searching on google, I found out my image location is wrong. Therefore, remember to crear a (image) folder under your source.
  • to add image into your blog, fitst in your _config.yml, change “post_asset_folder: false” to true. Next, in your post, use markdown synatx ![]( place your absolute image path)
