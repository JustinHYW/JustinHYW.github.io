Basic Computer Science for Biginner

Despite I am not a real computer science student, but I believe everyone who is in this field shoudl know a bit of computer science.

What is Computer Science

Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems. Unlike electrical and computer engineers, computer scientists deal mostly with software and software systems; this includes their theory, design, development, and application.

[University of Maryland] [https://undergrad.cs.umd.edu/what-computer-science]

There are many computer science disciplines out there, such as Applied Mathematics, Computer Engineering, Operating Systems, and Data Management, etc. How we can choose them it really depend on yout interest.

In this series, I will be introduce some basic computer science knowledge for biginner. You must have a solid foundation if you’re going to have a strong superstructure. Another reason why we should learn some CS knowledge is that many tech companies will ask you some related questions during the interview. Having a broad knowledge can help you stand out. We may don’t have to master all of them; at least we have to know about them.
